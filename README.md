# 🎯 TalentScout AI 

**End-to-End Workflow: From Candidate Application to AI-Powered Decision & Engagement**

TalentScout AI is an automated, intelligent recruitment platform. It leverages Google Workspace (Sheets/Drive), an AI Analysis Engine, and a custom-built HR Dashboard to seamlessly ingest applications, evaluate candidates against Job Descriptions, and provide data-driven recommendations to recruiters.

---

## 🏗 System Architecture & Workflow

The pipeline consists of 8 core stages:

1. **Candidate Applies:** Candidates submit an application including their personal details, experience, resume upload, role motivation, company motivation, and past projects.
2. **Data Ingestion:** Application form data is saved into Google Sheets. Uploaded resumes (PDF/Doc) are automatically stored in Google Drive.
3. **Resume Processing:** Text is extracted from the resumes. The system cleans, parses, and extracts raw skills, projects, and work experience.
4. **JD Parsing:** The system loads Job Descriptions from a `JD_Config`. It extracts the core required skills, bonus skills, and the target role level.
5. **AI Analysis Engine:** 
   * Performs semantic skill matching.
   * Infers hidden skills based on described projects and experiences.
   * Calculates Match/Scores and generates readable reasoning for its decisions.
6. **Scoring & Decision:** Synthesizes scores into an automated decision engine (Shortlist vs. Reject / Priority levels).
7. **HR Review (Our Dashboard):** HR reviews the AI output on the TalentScout UI, updates states (Approve / Reject), and syncs the final decision back to the sheet.
8. **Candidate Engagement:** 
   * **If Approved:** Sends selection email, shares take-home assignment link, and defines deadline.
   * **If Rejected:** Sends rejection email with personalized, constructive feedback generated by AI.

## 📊 Scoring Framework

TalentScout uses a multi-dimensional scoring framework to evaluate overall candidate fitness.

### A. Match Score (0 - 100)
Measures how well the candidate's explicit/inferred skills match the **Required Skills** in the job description.
> `Match Score = (Required Skills Matched / Total Required Skills) × 100`

### B. Bonus Score (0 - 100)
Measures how many "good-to-have" or bonus skills the candidate possesses.
> `Bonus Score = (Bonus Skills Matched / Total Bonus Skills) × 100`

### C. Interest Score (0 - 100)
A holistic score measuring a candidate's motivation, intent, and cultural fit.
* **Match Contribution (30%):** Weighted directly from the calculated Match Score.
* **Motivation Score (40%):** AI-rated score (0-100) based on textual responses to "Why this role?" and "Why this company?".
* **Consistency Score (30%):** AI-rated score evaluating how logically their past projects and experiences align with the applied role.

## 🧠 Decision Engine (Logic Trees)

The AI Decision Engine handles applications differently based on the candidate's experience level.

### For Experienced Roles
1. **Is Match Score ≥ 75%?**
   * **NO:** ❌ REJECT *(Not enough primary skills match)*
   * **YES:** Proceeds to Bonus evaluation.
     * **Is Match + Bonus ≥ 150?**
     * **YES:** ✅ SHORTLIST *(High Priority)*
     * **NO:** ✅ SHORTLIST *(Low Priority)*

### For Fresher / 0 Years Experience
1. **Is Match Score ≥ 90%?**
   * **NO:** ❌ REJECT *(Needs more skills/foundation)*
   * **YES:** ✅ SHORTLIST *(Fresher Override)*

### 🚦 Priority Tiers
* **Highest Priority:** Match ≥ 85% AND (Match + Bonus) ≥ 160
* **High Priority:** Match ≥ 75% AND (Match + Bonus) ≥ 150
* **Low Priority:** Match ≥ 60%
* **Reject:** Match < 60%

## 🔍 AI Skill Matching Logic

Rather than relying on basic keyword searches, the engine uses contextual Natural Language Processing:

* **Semantic Matching:** Matches skills by meaning. *(Example: Recognizing that a candidate saying "Pandas" matches the JD requirement for "Python Data Analysis".)*
* **Skill Inference:** Deduced from behaviors. *(Example: "Built interactive dashboards for clients" = Data Visualization skill inferred.)*
* **Categorized Matching:** Separates core requirements from "nice-to-have" tools/technologies.

## 🚀 Key Benefits

* ✅ **Fair & Transparent Evaluation:** Eliminates preliminary human bias by relying on standard criteria.
* ✅ **Reduces Manual Screening Effort:** HR only looks at top-tier candidates instead of thousands of resumes.
* ✅ **Data-Driven Hiring Decisions:** Detailed metric breakdowns for every yes/no decision.
* ✅ **Better Candidate Experience:** Rapid response times and automated, personalized feedback for rejected candidates.
* ✅ **Highly Adaptable:** Handles complex criteria for experienced hires while allowing strict overrides for freshers.
